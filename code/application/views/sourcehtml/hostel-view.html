À propos de nom de l'hostel

Hotel Lido has 14 rooms, and 1 suite. The hotel rooms, in its classic style, offer for our guests a very relaxing environment with Air Conditioner, Refrigerator, Satellite TV, Direct dial Phone with international connection, Wireless Internet Access (Free of charge), WC, Toilet, Shower. Hotel Lido offers a variety of services, which make the stay very comfortable for our guests: Accommodation in Single Room, Double Room and Suite, Room Service, Taxi Service (by request), Laundry Service EXTRA, Hall, bars, Restaurant, Money Exchange, Meeting Room, Conference Room, Parties... etc. Free Wireless Internet Access 24/7, Direct Dial Telephone (Including International Connection), Secretary Services, Photocopy and FAX.

A credit card is required to secure the reservation.

You have up to 3 days prior to arrival to cancel the reservation without charge. If the reservation is cancelled within 72 hours prior to arrival you will be charged 1 night stay. Same day cancellations, no shows or early check out will result in 1 night charge.
